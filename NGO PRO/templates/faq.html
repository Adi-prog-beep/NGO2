Common Questions

HopeHands NGO is dedicated to improving the lives of elderly people by providing care, medical assistance, and volunteer programs that allow them to lead dignified, healthy lives.

You can become a volunteer by visiting our 'Get Involved' section and filling out the volunteer form. We welcome individuals who are passionate about helping the elderly.

You can donate online through our 'Donate' page. We accept one-time and recurring donations to support our various programs for the elderly.

We organize fundraising events, volunteer training workshops, and community outreach activities to engage the public and support our elderly programs.
