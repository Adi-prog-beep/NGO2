Important Safety Tips for Elders
  • Always save emergency numbers on your phone or in a visible place.
  • Keep a list of your medical conditions and medications nearby.
  • Know your nearest hospital, doctor, and emergency shelter locations.
  • Stay connected with family and caregivers regularly.
